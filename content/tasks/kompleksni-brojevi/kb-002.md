## Zadatak

Neka je $z=3-2i$.

**a)** Izračunati $z\cdot\bar z$, $\dfrac{\bar z}{z}$ i $\dfrac{1}{z}$.

**b)** Naći realni i imaginarni deo broja $w=\dfrac{4+7i}{2+i}+\dfrac{1-3i}{1+i}$.

## Rešenje

**a)** $z\bar z=|z|^2=3^2+(-2)^2=13$. Dalje

$$\frac{\bar z}{z}=\frac{3+2i}{3-2i}=\frac{(3+2i)^2}{13}=\frac{5+12i}{13},\qquad \frac{1}{z}=\frac{\bar z}{|z|^2}=\frac{3+2i}{13}.$$

**b)** $\dfrac{4+7i}{2+i}=\dfrac{(4+7i)(2-i)}{5}=\dfrac{15+10i}{5}=3+2i$; $\dfrac{1-3i}{1+i}=\dfrac{(1-3i)(1-i)}{2}=\dfrac{-2-4i}{2}=-1-2i$.

Zbir: $w=(3+2i)+(-1-2i)=2$, pa je $\operatorname{Re}w=2$, $\operatorname{Im}w=0$.
