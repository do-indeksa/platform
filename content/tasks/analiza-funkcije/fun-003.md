## Zadatak

Izračunati određene integrale:

**a)** $\displaystyle\int_{1}^{e} x\ln x\,dx$ (parcijalna integracija);

**b)** $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{1} x\sqrt{1+x^2}\,dx$ (smenom);

**c)** $\displaystyle\int_{1}^{e}\frac{\ln x}{x}\,dx$ (smenom) i $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{\pi/2} x\sin x\,dx$ (parcijalna integracija).

## Rešenje

**a)** Parcijalnom integracijom $u=\ln x$, $dv=x\,dx$:

$$\int_{1}^{e} x\ln x\,dx=\frac{x^2}{2}\ln x\Big|_{1}^{e}-\int_{1}^{e}\frac{x}{2}\,dx=\frac{e^2}{2}-\left(\frac{e^2}{4}-\frac14\right)=\frac{e^2+1}{4}.$$

**b)** Smena $u=1+x^2$, $x\,dx=\tfrac12\,du$; granice $1\to 2$:

$$\int_{0}^{1} x\sqrt{1+x^2}\,dx=\frac12\int_{1}^{2} u^{1/2}\,du=\frac13 u^{3/2}\Big|_{1}^{2}=\frac{2\sqrt{2}-1}{3}.$$

**c)** Smena $t=\ln x$: $\displaystyle\int_{1}^{e}\frac{\ln x}{x}\,dx=\int_{0}^{1} t\,dt=\frac12$. Parcijalnom integracijom $u=x$, $dv=\sin x\,dx$:

$$\int_{0}^{\pi/2} x\sin x\,dx=-x\cos x\Big|_{0}^{\pi/2}+\sin x\Big|_{0}^{\pi/2}=1.$$
